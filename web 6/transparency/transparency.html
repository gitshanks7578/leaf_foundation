Governance Overview

  • Organizational Structure

    Our NGO is governed by a Board of Trustees. The Board comprises dedicated individuals who serve as the President, Vice President, Secretary, and Executive Members. Together, they bring a mix of experience, expertise, and passion for social good.

  • Decision-Making Process

    Major decisions are taken collectively, through discussion and mutual agreement among the Trustees. This collaborative approach ensures that policies and strategies are well thought out and reflect the values of inclusivity and fairness. The Board is designed to consist of a minimum of seven and a maximum of fifteen Trustees, ensuring both diversity and efficiency in leadership.

  • Long-Term Commitment

    The Trust is irrevocable, which means its charitable objectives cannot be altered or dissolved for private benefit. This ensures that the organization remains committed to its mission of public service and continues to operate in line with the highest legal and ethical standards.

Legal and Ethical Commitment

  • Statutory Compliance

    All financial and administrative practices are carried out in full accordance with the laws of India. Funds are exclusively utilized for charitable and developmental activities within the country, and all operations are subject to the scrutiny of statutory bodies.

  • Ethical Practices

    Beyond compliance with the law, we hold ourselves accountable to a higher standard of ethics. Integrity and honesty form the foundation of every decision we make. Whether in how we use funds, how we communicate with stakeholders, or how we treat the communities we serve, transparency is embedded in every action.

  • Public Benefit First

    Every project, every initiative, and every rupee spent is dedicated to creating lasting public benefit. We believe that resources entrusted to us are not just donations but responsibilities, and it is our duty to ensure they are used effectively to bring about sustainable social change.
