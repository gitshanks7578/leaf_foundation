About us

overview

Founded on May 14, 2025, by Girish Kumar Tiwari, The Leaf Foundation is a public charitable trust based in Aligarh. Our mission is to drive societal upliftment and promote sustainable development, creating opportunities for an inclusive and empowered society. The Foundation is headquartered at Ramghat Road, Aligarh-202001, with the flexibility to expand as guided by the Trustees.

Our values

At the heart of The Leaf Foundation lie three guiding values that shape every initiative we undertake :
  • Inclusivity –

    We believe true progress can only be achieved when everyone is part of the journey. From marginalized communities to underrepresented voices, our work ensures no one is left behind, fostering unity and belonging in every project.

  • Empowerment –

    Real change begins when people have the tools, knowledge, and confidence to transform their own lives. We focus on equipping individuals and communities with education, resources, and opportunities so they can step forward as leaders of tomorrow..

  • Ethical Development –

    Growth without ethics is unsustainable. Every program we design is rooted in transparency, responsibility, and fairness, ensuring that the impact we create is not only meaningful today but also sustainable for generations to come.
